Role-based onboarding

Create the exact profile the backend expects.

After Google login, KaamSetu requires a role-specific profile. This page submits a payload matching POST /profile/setup-role for workers, employers, contractors, and local operators.

  • Worker: skills, rates, service radius, languages, availability.
  • Employer: household, shop, business, farm, or event organizer setup.
  • Contractor: work categories, frequent locations, bulk hiring.
  • Operator: assigned area and verification permissions.
